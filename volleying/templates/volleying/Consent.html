Northeastern University, Department of Marketing:

Name of Investigator(s): Mary Steffel

Title of Project: Choice Delegation

Request to Participate in Research

We would like to invite you to participate in a web-based online survey. You must be at least 18 years old to take this survey.

Key Information

  • Your consent is being sought for participation in a research project and your participation is voluntary.
  • The survey is part of a research study whose purpose is to understand when people delegate choices to other people and the consequences of having the option to delegate.
  • This survey should take about 10 minutes to complete.
  • The procedures that you will be asked to complete include reading scenarios, making choices and answering general demographic questions.
  • There are no foreseeable risks or discomforts to you for taking part in this study.
  • There are no direct benefits to you from participating in this study. However, your responses may help us learn more about when people delegate choices to other people and the consequences of having the option to delegate.

The decision to participate in this research project is voluntary. You do not have to participate and you can refuse to answer any question. Even if you begin the web-based online survey, you can stop at any time. You will receive Marketing Research Experience (MRE) credit for completing the survey.

Your part in this study will be handled in a confidential manner. Any reports or publications based on this research will use only group data and will not identify you or any individual as being affiliated with this project.

Your de-identified information could be used for future research without additional informed consent.
